National Symbols and Date and Time

There are many things about Dubai and the surrounding Arabian Emirates that separates them from other nations, there national animal is no exception. If you combined a goat and a unicorn you might get something like this creature. A perfect representation of Dubai. Dubai in the worlds eyes would be considered a "Unicorn". This is because of how special it is and unique its cities, people, and animals are.

This is the UAE national symbol, of which Dubai is a member. The falcon is a representation of power, history, and exceptional agility. The falcon assumes an alert, ready, and solidly planted stance as it stands. The UAE flag has seven stars, which stand for the seven Emirates, are connected by an unbreakable ring to form a single entity.

Dubai's time zone is Gulf Standard time. In comparison to ours they are about 6 hours ahead of us . Dubai also does not observe Daylight Saving Time clock changes. This is due to their location on the earth, as they are closer to the center it allows them to receive sunlight at more consistent times.
